Why do Employers Prefer our Graduates?

1. Proven Performance

 Upon enrolling, our students are immersed into assignments and projects that replicate tasks they will encounter in the real business world. Our students are prepared to thrive in the work world by training in it. A VPi student graduates with 2-4 years of real-world business experience and 1.5-3 years of exceptional internships. All this creates a prove track-record of real-world job performance that employers trust.

2. Drive for Excellence

VPi cultivates a culture of excellence among our students. Excellence is drilled into our students by their mentor-managers, readings, and assignments. Our students resolve each day to strive to raise the definition of excellence.

3. Impressive Credentials

VPi students graduate with credentials that go well beyond the average graduate attempting to enter the workforce. The resumes of our graduates are bolstered with impressive internships, association with ivy league schools, recognized certifications, international experience.

4. Teachable and Innovative

In today’s economy, business owners need individuals who are gifted problem-solvers, those who can get the job done, do more with less, and find a better way. At the same time, idea people often struggle to follow directions and do things someone else’s way. We have sought to instill the best of both worlds in our students. Heavy emphasis on both teachability and problem-solving skills define our curriculum.

Why do Students Choose Our Program?

The risk of traditional preparation for the work world continues to rise. Parents and students alike are beginning to realize that spending tens of thousands of dollars and 4+ years of life is not worth a 50/50 chance at a better job. VPi has developed our program to help students reduce or eliminate this risk. Here are some of the top reasons students are choosing to use our program:

1. Employment

The Van Pelt Institute stands by its commitment to help our students find excellent employment opportunities. We accomplish this with our five-fold employment assurance strategy. Everything we do at VPi is centered around our mission of maintaining a 100% employment rate among our graduates.

2. Save Money and Avoid Waste

Four years of VPi costs less than one year at the average higher education institution and full-time students finish in a little over two years. Our program was designed to reduce the risk students must take to pursue career advancement. Instead of paying tens of thousands to learn information of little value, students receive an intensive focused education that truly prepares them for the business world.

3. Excellence in Economic Ability and Versatility

Students who come to VPi are tired with the failing college system. They are discouraged with wasting thousands of dollars and hours on education that fails to provide the skills, knowledge, and experience necessary for success. These students are driven. They understand the volatility of our current economic system and the importance of being economically prepared. They desire to develop economic stability and become the definition of excellence in their given field.
